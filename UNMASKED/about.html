Shallowness of society has been created
through the emerging advancement of technology
that could eventually take over a waster
part our lives. The phenomenon is shown
through the changing habits and environmental
landscape in our world. Social media
platforms like Instagram have become an integrated
aspect of our everyday lives, shorter attention
spans, as well as more shallow attitudes
are prevailing. In-depth readings and deep
connections within humans are decreasing; this
could eventually become a challenge for us to
overcome in order to maintain our ideal mental
health. Regarding the increased superficiality,
the main focus is on examining its existence, its
effects and to eventually bring out the realness
that remains underneath it all in this photography
and text based publication.
